About Howell Holdings

Howell Holdings LLC is a Delaware limited liability company formed on June 16, 2025, serving as the corporate trustee for The Howell Legacy Group, a sophisticated Delaware statutory trust structure. Operating under the DBA "AITCH," we provide comprehensive fiduciary oversight and strategic management of all trust assets and business operations.

As corporate trustee, Howell Holdings LLC fulfills a critical fiduciary role, ensuring proper asset management and protection for trust beneficiaries while maintaining the highest standards of corporate governance and regulatory compliance. Our structure enables seamless succession planning and continuity of family enterprise operations across multiple generations.

Organizational Structure

Corporate Hierarchy

Howell Holdings LLC serves as the sole member and manager of AITCH Enterprises LLC, which functions as the primary asset holding company for all operational subsidiaries. This three-tier structure (Trust → Corporate Trustee → Holding Company → Operating Subsidiaries) provides optimal asset protection, tax efficiency, and governance flexibility.

Management

John Shelton Howell III serves as Managing Trustee and Manager, with full authority for strategic direction, subsidiary oversight, capital allocation, and all major business decisions. The management structure ensures continuity through clearly defined succession protocols and bloodline-only trustee requirements.

Key Operating Divisions

Technology: AITCH Technologies LLC manages a portfolio of 10 patent-holding LLCs with non-provisional applications in sustainable building systems, agricultural innovation, and environmental technology.

Financial Services: 52nd Street Capital Group LLC provides investment management, wealth planning, and fiduciary services with integrated Solo 401(k) retirement plan administration.

Real Estate: AITCH Land Group LLC handles strategic real estate investments, development projects, and property management operations.

Entertainment: AITCH IP Holdings LLC, 360 World Music LLC, and Global Music Group LLC manage music publishing, production, and international distribution.

Charitable Operations: Three independent 501(c)(3) foundations (Today's Child, Ifoma, and AITCH Conservation) operate at arm's length to preserve tax-exempt status.
